Meet Nini

A cook, a forager, a grandmother's granddaughter.

I grew up watching my grandmother turn three humble ingredients into something worth writing home about. That's still how I cook — with patience, good butter, and whatever the farmers brought in that week.

After a decade in restaurant kitchens from Boston to Lisbon, I traded the chaos of service for the warmth of home kitchens. Now I cook for families, couples, and the occasional lucky dinner party. Always organic. Always from scratch. Never complicated for the sake of it.

— Nini

The Menu of Services

However you like to eat, I'll cook around it.

Every package is customized — dietary needs, pantry preferences, kids who hate mushrooms, the whole lot. Pricing below is a starting point; your quote will be tailored after our discovery call.

01 — Weekly Meal Prep

The Weekday Rescue

Five nights of dinners, prepped and labeled in your fridge each Sunday. Think: braised short ribs, lemony grain bowls, roasted-vegetable tartines. Reheating instructions included.

5 dinners · serves 2–4 from $385/wk
02 — In-Home Dinners

Supper, Served

I arrive with groceries, cook in your kitchen, plate each course, and leave it spotless. A three-course tasting for two, or a six-course feast for ten. You just pour the wine.

2–12 guests · 3–6 courses from $145/pp
03 — Dinner Parties & Events

The Gathering

Birthdays, anniversaries, Sunday brunches, backyard harvest dinners. Fully custom menus with seasonal produce, curated wine pairings on request, and optional service staff.

up to 40 guests custom quote
04 — Private Lessons

Cooking, Together

Learn the techniques I use every day — perfect risotto, proper pan sauces, a loaf of sourdough you'll actually bake again. Hands-on, in your kitchen, at your pace.

2–4 hours · 1–4 students from $225/hr
05 — Signature Offering

The Full-Season Subscription

A twelve-week commitment where I become your household chef — weekly menus built around your calendar, dietary goals, and whatever's peaking at the farm. Includes a monthly dinner party at no extra cost.
